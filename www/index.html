About

Seeds of Consciousness was born from the mission of its founder, which is to expand the collective consciousness and help people to awaken to their true self. It’s a program that incorporates multiple projects, involving: meditation, yoga, spirituality, sustainability and coaching. It’s also a community where we get together to learn, inspire each other and grow from a place of inner wisdom and love.

We have four pillars that are our foundation. It’s the road map we follow to design every course, event, service or product of our business, which are: Commitment to Wellbeing, Education, Personal Development (Growth) and Consciousness. Our pillars form our own and unique identity, the things we essentially hold and account in order to create anything.

5 Week Transformation Journey Through Meditation

Grounded in the traditional meditation techniques of Classical Yoga Philosophy and based on studies of neuroscience, you will not only explore a variety of effective meditation styles, but also learn valuable yoga skills and science to support the practice of meditation.


It’s also part of this program, questioning materials focused on personal development and habit life science, that will support you throughout the course, giving you more clarity and strategies to build effective habits.


It contains theory for the intellectual, art for the visionary, meditations for the ethereal and exercises for the body. A program that we call a personal grow path to your sacred self.


At the end of the program you will have the tools and techniques that prepare for and enhance meditation, giving you the ability to manage independently your practice and to take mindfulness off the meditation cushion and into daily life.
